Digital Audio Visualizer

Project Dates: October 2025 - Present

Goals:

  • Digital Design
  • System Verilog
  • RTL Design
  • FPGA Design
  • Signal Processing
  • Fourier Transform

This project involves creating a digital audio visualizer using System Verilog and FPGA design techniques. The goal is to process audio signals and generate corresponding visual patterns in real-time.

By utilizing the Fourier Transform, the visualizer can analyze the frequency components of the audio signal and create dynamic visual effects that correspond to the music being played.

This project is ongoing, and I am currently learning System Verilog and digital design basics to understand implementing designs on my Basys 3 FPGA.
