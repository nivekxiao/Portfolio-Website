Drone

Project Dates: January 2025 - March 2025

Goals:

  • 3D Printing
  • Motor Control
  • Design

This drone was designed using using a custom 3D printed frame designed for speed. We chose specific blades that were the most efficient during testing, and wired the motors to the circuit board and powered it with a battery. The board was then connected to a controller via QGroundControl and flown. During this class, our drones had to be designed for a specific task, and ours was speed. We chose the barebones design, light custom frame, and blades with this purpose in mind.

This project was completed in UCLA's ENGR 1DR class in collaboration with three other classmates.
