PitcherVC

Project Dates: January 2024

Goals:

  • UI Design
  • Presenting
  • Startup Ideas

PitcherVC is a mobile app that allows startups to spread their businesses and venture capitalists to easily swipe through startups in an easy-to-use format.

The app connects startups through LinkedIn, and allows them to post 30-second pitches for their companies. VCs can then easily scroll through startups and swipe right to save a startup they like or swipe left to pass on a particular startup. VCs can then connect to the startups they swiped right on through LinkedIn.

This project was co-designed with Tyler Bordeaux at Michigan State University during MLH SpartaHack 9. Tyler handled the software behind creating the app's functionality, while I helped with UI design, brainstorming, and presenting the project to the hackathon's judges.
